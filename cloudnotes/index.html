Warning

If you clear your cookies, you will loose your data. That's why it's good to download a backup copy every once in a while.

Data

CloudNotes stores your data in your browser. We choose to make your data private. We do not have any access to your notes. In fact, that data is not even sent through the internet. It lives on your computer and your computer only.

Yes, in that sense, CloudNotes is a misnomer.
